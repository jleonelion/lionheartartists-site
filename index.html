Our Story

Where Dreams Take Center Stage

LionHeart Artists was founded by Lisa Leone with a simple belief: every actor deserves someone in their corner who sees their potential and fiercely protects their spark.

Based in Los Angeles, we're a boutique talent management firm specializing in performers ages 4–18. We offer representation, mentoring and coaching, audition preparation, and career guidance. We represent talent across the board: from print and commercial to tv/film, voiceover and influencer personalities as well!

Our intentionally small roster means your family isn't just another name on a list. We get to know your child, understand their dreams, and walk alongside you every step of the way, because behind every young star is a family who believed, encouraged, and supported them.

"Success in this industry isn't just about booking roles. It's about building careers with integrity, relationships with heart, and futures with purpose."
— Lisa Leone, Founder

The Magic of the Set

There's nothing quite like watching a child walk onto a set for the first time — eyes wide, heart pounding, ready to create something extraordinary.

We prepare our young talent not just for the audition, but for the moment the cameras roll and onto the red carpet. From understanding the rhythm of a professional set to building genuine relationships with directors and crew, your child will feel confident, prepared, and excited, because when kids feel safe and supported, that's when the real magic happens.

Family First, Always

At LionHeart, we never lose sight of what matters most: your child's well-being and your family's peace of mind.

We educate parents on the realities of the industry, keep communication open and honest, and make sure every opportunity aligns with your family's values. No surprises, no pressure — just a trusted partner who's as invested in your actor's happiness and success as you are.

This isn't just business. It's a relationship built on trust, heart, and shared purpose.
